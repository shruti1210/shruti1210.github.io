It is uneasy, confusing and lonely

Shruti wrote this on Tue, 23 April 2019

A whole new place, .. but slowly

A few beads of thoughts

It is uneasy, confusing and lonely, with a set of people around and you are not sure if you belong there. It is a feeling of loneliness and with a cluster of people around you. The lonely yourself, scared and counting twice before filling up on each of the formalities. All new faces, all new people, not a single strand attached. A whole new place, the new colour of the walls, the new trees, the new temple, and above all someone new to share your life. A whole new set of people, and your difference, but slowly, the arrogant lady turned into comfort for your illness, or the unapproachable professors became your guides. The unknown faces and heartless handshakes turned in to hugs, the initial awkward conversations turned into deep heartfelt pain shared and just forgotten by mornings. Through the whole old reluctant self, cautious about how much you are allowed to eat in mess, to try to eat sometimes there, from random first interactions to unspoken exchanges, from being away from people to just being there for them, it has all passed. And it all suddenly, every corner, every corner of campus feels like you belong here, it is where you are from.

The connecting string!

It represents that basic contrast you face when you move into a new place, and for me it was moving away from home to university dormitory or to a far away country for my internships.
